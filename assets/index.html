Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes, our basic lessons and most interactive features are free for everyone to help you start your journey!

3. Do I need to create an account?

Creating an account is recommended to track your progress, save your favorite lessons, and unlock personalized recommendations.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

You can use our dedicated vocabulary section, where words are grouped by levels, along with meanings and correct pronunciations.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, upon completing our specific assessment paths or structured courses, you can earn a digital certificate of achievement.
